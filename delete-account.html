Igloo Parking App - Account Deletion

You can delete your Igloo Parking account directly from the app.

Steps

  1. Open Igloo Parking App
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion

Data Deleted

  • User name
  • Email
  • Phone number
  • Parking history
  • Saved vehicles
  • Location preferences

Data Retention

Transaction records may be stored up to 90 days for legal purposes.

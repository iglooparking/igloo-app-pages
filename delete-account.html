Account Deletion Request

Delete Your Account

Users can permanently delete their Igloo Parking account directly from inside the mobile application.

Steps to Delete Account

  1. Open the Igloo Parking App
  2. Open the Side Menu / Sidebar
  3. Go to Profile or Settings
  4. Select Delete Account
  5. Confirm the account deletion request

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Saved vehicles
  • Parking history
  • Location preferences

Data Retention

Some transaction records may be retained for up to 90 days to comply with financial and legal obligations.

Need Help?

If you cannot access your account in the app, you can request deletion by contacting our support team.
